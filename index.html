Step 1: Read the directions on the package carefuly and follow it.

Advanced Cup Noodles

  • Time: ~5 min.
  • Serves: 1 bowl
Ingredients Quantity
cup noodles your preference
beef cuts your preference
Watercress several leaves
tomatos one

Step 1: Cut the tomato in small slices and put them with stacked noodles in a pot.(don't forget to add the flavor bags),
Step 2: Pour water in the pot till it reaches the top of the noodles.
Step 3: Cook for around 5 minutes at large fire.
Step 4: Take noodles out and put in a bowl, put the tamatos on the side and the cooked beef slices on the middle
Step 5: Add the Watercress leaves (toppings) on the top of the beef cuts.
